📘 What is NeurAloud?

AI-powered multilingual reading app supporting PDF, DOCX, EPUB, and text files.

🧠 Features

  • PDF highlight and reading overlay
  • Library and Playlist with drag-drop
  • 1 hour free TTS daily

🎧 Supported Voices

  • 100+ free voices across languages
  • Custom pitch, rate, loop & auto-resume

📲 Platform

  • Responsive Web + PWA support
  • Offline mode via Service Worker
